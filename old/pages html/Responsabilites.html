Our Vision of Ecology

We want our designs to last – both in terms of function and aesthetics. In our quest to achieve this, we employ strict sustainability criteria and work within a set framework of practices. We take a 'cradle-to-grave' approach to consider the entire life cycle of our products, and work to ensure that as many of our products as possible are made from responsible materials.
